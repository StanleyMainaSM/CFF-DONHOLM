About CFF Donholm

Our Mission

"Let heaven fill your mind"

At CFF Donholm, we are dedicated to creating a spiritual haven where believers can encounter God's presence, grow in faith, and make a positive impact in their communities. We believe in the transformative power of the Gospel and the importance of living out our faith in practical ways.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of hope and light in Donholm and beyond, leading people to a deeper relationship with Jesus Christ and empowering them to live purposeful, God-centered lives.

What We Believe

  • In the power of Jesus Christ to transform lives
  • In the authority and truth of God's Word (the Bible)
  • In the importance of community and fellowship
  • In serving others with compassion and integrity
  • In the Holy Spirit's guidance in all we do

Our Story

CFF Donholm was established with a passion to create a welcoming church community in the heart of Donholm. Over the years, we have grown into a vibrant fellowship of believers who are committed to worshiping God, serving one another, and reaching out to our community with the love of Christ.
