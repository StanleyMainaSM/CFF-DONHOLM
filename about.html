About CFF Donholm

Christian Foundation Fellowship - Donholm

Our Identity

Christian Foundation Fellowship (CFF) Donholm is a dynamic, Spirit-filled church community located in Donholm, Nairobi, Kenya. We are dedicated to the worship of Jesus Christ, the proclamation of God's Word, and the transformation of lives through faith and fellowship.

Our Mission Statement

"Let heaven fill your mind"

Colossians 3:1-3

Our mission is to create a welcoming church community where believers can encounter God's presence, grow in their faith, make disciples, and impact their communities with the love of Christ. We are committed to spreading the Gospel and transforming lives through faith in Jesus Christ.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of hope and light in Donholm and beyond, leading people to a deeper relationship with Jesus Christ and empowering them to live purposeful, God-centered lives that glorify His name and advance His Kingdom.

What We Believe

  • ✓ In the power of Jesus Christ to transform lives and set people free from sin
  • ✓ In the authority and truth of God's Word (the Bible) as our foundation for faith and practice
  • ✓ In the importance of community and fellowship as believers grow and support one another
  • ✓ In serving others with compassion and integrity as we demonstrate Christ's love
  • ✓ In the Holy Spirit's guidance and empowerment in all that we do and say

Our Story

CFF Donholm was established with a passion to create a welcoming, Bible-centered church community in the heart of Donholm. Over the years, we have grown into a vibrant fellowship of believers committed to worshiping God, studying His Word, and serving our community with Christ-like love.

Our church is characterized by dynamic worship, expository preaching, caring fellowship, and authentic discipleship. We believe that every member of our congregation has a role to play in God's Kingdom, and we are committed to equipping and empowering believers for service.

Our Core Values

🙏 Worship

We center our lives around worshiping God with authenticity, passion, and reverence, recognizing His supremacy and glory.

📖 Biblical Truth

We are committed to the study, teaching, and application of God's Word as the authority for our faith and life.

👥 Community

We foster a loving, supportive community where believers can grow together, care for one another, and experience genuine fellowship.

🚀 Discipleship

We are passionate about making disciples who follow Jesus closely and help others grow in their faith.

🤝 Compassion

We demonstrate Christ's love through service, care, and justice, meeting the needs of our community with genuine concern.

🎯 Excellence

We strive to do all things with excellence as an offering to God, honoring Him in our work and ministry.

Our Leadership

CFF Donholm is led by a dedicated pastoral team under the leadership of Pastor Daniel Gitahi, who is committed to shepherding the flock with biblical integrity and pastoral care. Our leadership team is passionate about serving God and His people with humility, wisdom, and devotion.

Our Programs & Ministries

🙏 Sunday Worship Services

Three worship services every Sunday with inspiring preaching and worship.

📅 Mid-Week Services

Multiple services throughout the week including morning devotions, prayer meetings, and specialized ministries.

👥 Small Groups & Fellowship

Community, youth, men's, and women's groups for deeper connection and discipleship.

📖 Bible Studies

In-depth Scripture studies for spiritual growth at all levels of faith.

🌍 Community Outreach

Mission and evangelism programs serving the Donholm community and beyond.

📺 Altime TV

Television and radio ministry broadcasting on YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, and 98.6 Kigooco FM.

Contact & Visit Us

We invite you to visit CFF Donholm and experience our welcoming church community for yourself. Whether you're exploring Christianity, looking for a church home, or simply curious about who we are, you'll find a warm reception and genuine fellowship.
