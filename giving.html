How Your Gifts Are Used

🙏 Worship & Discipleship

Supporting worship services, Bible studies, and spiritual development programs that strengthen our congregation's faith.

👥 Community Outreach

Reaching out through service projects, mission work, and compassion ministries that demonstrate God's love.

🏗️ Facility Maintenance

Maintaining and improving our church building and facilities to provide a welcoming space for worship.

📢 Media & Outreach

Supporting Altime TV and online ministry to reach people through YouTube, Instagram, and radio broadcasts.

Our Giving Principles

  • ✅ Voluntary Giving: All giving is voluntary and no one is coerced
  • ✅ Wise Stewardship: Funds are used wisely and with integrity
  • ✅ Transparency: Financial records are maintained with full accountability
  • ✅ Confidentiality: Your giving is treated with respect and confidentiality
  • ✅ Gratitude: Every donation is deeply appreciated and valued
