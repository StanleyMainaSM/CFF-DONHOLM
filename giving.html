Support Our Ministry

Why Support CFF Donholm?

Your generous gifts enable us to continue our mission of spiritual growth, community service, and outreach. Every contribution, regardless of size, makes a meaningful difference in transforming lives through the Gospel.

Ways to Give

💰 Cash Giving

Give during our Sunday services or Wednesday Bible studies. Offering plates are available throughout the service.

📱 Mobile Money

Send your offering via M-Pesa, Airtel Money, or other mobile payment options. Contact us for account details: 0720 924750

🏦 Bank Transfer

Make a direct bank transfer to support our ministry. Please call us for our banking details.

💻 Online Giving

Interested in setting up recurring online donations? Please contact us to discuss available options.

How Your Gifts Are Used

🙏 Worship & Discipleship

Supporting worship services, Bible studies, and spiritual development programs.

👥 Community Outreach

Reaching out to the community through service projects and compassion ministries.

🏗️ Facility Maintenance

Maintaining and improving our church building and facilities.

📊 Administration

Supporting operational costs and ministry management.

🤝 Mission & Missions

Supporting local and global mission initiatives.

👦 Youth & Family

Investing in youth programs and family ministry initiatives.

Our Giving Principles

  • ✅ All giving is voluntary and no one is coerced to give
  • ✅ Funds are used wisely and transparently
  • ✅ Financial records are maintained with integrity
  • ✅ Your giving is treated with confidentiality and respect
  • ✅ Every donation is appreciated and valued

Questions About Giving?

If you have questions about giving or need more information, please don't hesitate to reach out.
